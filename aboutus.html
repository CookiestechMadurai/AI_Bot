About Us

Tutoapp is an innovative platform designed to empower students and professionals in developing their communication skills through AI-driven mock interviews, public speaking simulations, and real-time voice assessments. Our platform delivers a realistic and supportive environment that helps users build confidence, improve articulation, and prepare effectively for real-world scenarios.

Whether you're gearing up for campus placements, competitive exams, or job interviews, Tutoapp is your personal practice ground—smart, intuitive, and results-driven.
